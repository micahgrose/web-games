# GYRE

*fall forever. catch a star. let go.*

A one-button momentum roguelite. You are a spark adrift in the dark — you never stop moving. **Hold** to catch the nearest star and swing around it; the longer and faster you orbit, the more the star *winds up*. **Release** to fling off along your arc and unleash a shockwave from the star — that wave is how you unmake the husks that hunt your light.

Reach the gate in each sector, gather light to deepen the spark, choose a gift, and fall to the heart of the gyre.

## Controls
- **Hold** (mouse / touch / **Space**) — catch a star and wind it up
- **Release** — fling off + release a shockwave (bigger the longer you wound it)
- **Dash** (**Shift** / right-click / two-finger tap) — break free when no star is near
- **Esc / P** pause &middot; **M** mute &middot; **1/2/3** pick a gift

## Stars rewrite the swing
- **Stars** (gold) — steady orbit
- **Pulsars** (cyan) — ever-accelerating; huge wind-up, hard to control
- **Voids** (violet) — pull you inward; dangerous, but a heavy blast
- **Brittle** (green) — shatter after one fling
- **Repulsors** (rose) — can't be caught; bounce off them

18 gifts including 4 evolutions, a procedural 5-sector descent, and a phased boss — **The Heart of the Gyre**, which only opens to a wave born off its own core.

Plain canvas + Web Audio. Single file, no libraries, no build step. Open `index.html`.
