# Lumen

A small spark, adrift in the deep.

Open `index.html` in any modern browser — desktop or phone. No build step, no
dependencies, nothing to install.

**Controls**

- Move with your mouse, your finger, or the arrow keys / WASD.
- `P` or `Esc` to pause, `M` to mute.

Everything you see and hear is drawn and synthesised on the fly; there are no
image or audio files. Your best run is remembered on your device.
