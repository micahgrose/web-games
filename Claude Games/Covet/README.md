# Covet

A one-minute arcade game. Gather the drifting light, carry it to the ring in the
center to bank it. The catch: a hoard is worth far more than the sum of its parts,
but the more you hold, the harder the dark hunts you — and a single touch scatters
everything you're carrying.

How greedy do you get before you cash out?

- **Move** — mouse, finger, or arrow keys / WASD
- **Bank** — step into the center ring
- **Pause** — P

Single self-contained `index.html`. No build, no dependencies. Open it anywhere —
desktop or mobile, even straight off the filesystem.
