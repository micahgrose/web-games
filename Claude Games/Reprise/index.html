How to play

Move into a foe to strike it (bump combat). Each loop gives you a fixed number of turns.

Echoes: when a loop ends (turns spent, you die, or you Rewind), time resets. Your actions return as an echo that repeats them exactly, in step with your new self. Stack echoes to be in many places at once.

Win a chamber when, within a single loop, your echoes + you clear the objective. You have a limited number of loops — spend them well.

Depth: Sentinels only break under two strikes the same turn. Pressure plates need a self standing on them to hold a door. Knock foes into pits and spikes. Cover all glyphs at once. Powers (blink, bolt, ward…) are recorded into echoes too.

Dying inside a loop only ends that loop — but a short echo helps less. The real clock is your loop budget.
