# Wend

*A maze tower defense across the ages.* Single-file (`index.html`) — open it in any browser.

## The twist
The horde always walks the **shortest road** from the gate to your keep. You don't place towers on a fixed track — you **bend the track** by where you build:

- **Towers & walls reroute the road.** Build a maze to force the long way through your kill-zone. A live ghost-path shows the new route (and how much longer it gets) *before* you commit — and you can never fully seal the road.
- **Traps sit ON the road and never reroute it.** Spikes, tar, fire glyphs, caltrops, mines, snares and warp runes line the path for guaranteed punishment.
- **Sub-grid footprints.** Walls are 1×1, most towers 2×2, heavy artillery 3×3. Highland gives +range; forest slows the horde; chasms are free walls; water blocks building.

## Depth
- **9 tower families, each evolving across 3 ages** — Medieval → Industrial → Modern (Archer→Repeater→Railgun, Catapult→Mortar→Artillery, Frost→Cryo→Stasis, and more: cannon, flame, poison, sniper, gold-mint economy, command-web support).
- **8 trap types** with distinct control effects.
- **~14 enemy types that each break the maze differently:** flyers ignore it entirely, burrowers tunnel through walls, sappers smash your walls, clerics heal, bulwarks raise front shields, oozes split, wraiths phase intangible, trolls regenerate.
- **Bosses every 10 levels**, hundreds of generated levels, and a **press-your-luck** early-wave bonus.
- **Persistent meta-progression** ("Halls of War"): spend relics on starting gold/lives, tower damage, trap power, salvage refund, and **grid-size expansion**.

## Controls
- Click a build in the side palette, then click the board to place it. Right-click / **Esc** cancels.
- Click a placed tower to inspect, **Evolve**, or **Sell**.
- **Space** start wave · **P** pause · **1–9 / 0** quick-select towers · speed 1× / 2× / 3×.
