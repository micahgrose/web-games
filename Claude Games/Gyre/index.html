GYRE

fall forever · catch a star · let go

You are a spark adrift in the dark. You never stop moving.

HOLD (mouse / touch / Space) to catch the nearest star and swing around it. The longer and faster you orbit, the more the star winds up.

RELEASE to fling off along your arc — and unleash a shockwave from the star, sized by how far you wound it. That wave is how you kill the husks that hunt your light.

DASH (Shift / right-click / two-finger tap) once charged, to break free when no star is near.

Reach the gate in each sector. Gather light. Choose a gift. Survive to the heart of the gyre.
