GYRE

fall forever · catch a star · let go

You are a spark adrift in the dark. You never stop moving.

HOLD (mouse / touch / Space) to catch the nearest star and swing around it. The longer you hold, the faster you swing and the deeper the star winds up.

RELEASE to fling off along your arc — and unleash a shockwave from the star, sized by how far you wound it.

BEACONS sleep in each sector. Only a wave of high wind-up can light one — a faint wave fizzles against it. Light them all and the gate opens.

The open gate only takes the swift — fling yourself through it at speed.

DASH (Shift / right-click / two-finger tap) to break free when no star is near.

Husks hunt your light. Waves unmake them. Fall to the heart of the gyre and still it.
