# Nyx

A small spark of light, alone in the dark, and the dark is hungry.

Open `index.html` in any browser — desktop or phone, even straight off the
filesystem. No build step, no dependencies, nothing to install.

**How to play**

- Drag (touch) or move the mouse — your spark follows. It fires on its own.
- Gather the falling light dropped by the things you destroy; fill the bar to grow.
- Every time you grow, choose one of three powers. They stack — build a spark
  that suits how you like to play (splitting beams, orbital shards, seekers,
  detonations, nova pulses…).
- Stay in motion. Contact hurts. The hull at the bottom is all you have.
- Outlast the abyss long enough and something far larger comes up to meet you.

Everything — the art, the sound, the music — is drawn and synthesised on the
fly; there are no image or audio files. Your best score is kept on your device.
