# Bastion

A turn-based tactics game about defending a handful of glowing cores against an
endless tide. You are outnumbered — but never surprised. Every enemy shows you
exactly where it will strike *before* it strikes.

Open `index.html` in any browser — desktop or phone, even straight off the
filesystem. No build step, no dependencies, nothing to install.

**How to play**

- You command two machines. Each turn, tap one to move it, then fire.
- Your weapons don't just damage — they **push**. Use that. Shove an attacker
  off its mark so its strike lands on empty ground, knock two enemies into each
  other, or pin them against a mountain or the board's edge.
- The **Striker** fires a bolt in one direction, shoving whatever it hits.
- The **Mortar** drops onto any tile and blasts all four neighbours outward.
- Red marks are enemy strikes queued for next turn — and they follow an enemy
  when you push it, so you can watch a threat move off your cores in real time.
- Lose a core and the Grid drops. At zero Grid, the line falls.
- `Reset Turn` rewinds your whole turn so you can plan freely. `End Turn`
  hands it to the enemy.

It never ends — the waves only grow heavier. The goal is to hold as long as you
can. Everything is drawn on the fly; no image or audio files. Your best score is
remembered on your device.
