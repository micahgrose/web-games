# Marigold

A quiet little puzzle about patience and order.

Open `index.html` in any browser — desktop or phone, even straight off the
filesystem. No build step, no dependencies, nothing to install.

**How to play**

- Tap (or click) a cluster of two or more touching tiles of the same color to clear it.
- Bigger clusters are worth far more than small ones, so it pays to grow a color
  before you pop it — the order you clear in is the whole game.
- Empty the board for a large bonus.
- `Undo` takes back your last move; `New board` deals a fresh one.

Everything is drawn and synthesised on the fly — no image or audio files. Your
best score is remembered on your device.
