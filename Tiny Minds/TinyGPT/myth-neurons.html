the myth

"A bigger network is always better."

✗ The myth: pile on neurons and it gets smarter.
✓ The truth: capacity only helps up to what the data needs. Past that, extra neurons just memorize, making new replies worse.
👀 Watch: Train — it auto-pauses at 1500 steps. Note the generalized bar and how far it sits below memorized.
🔁 Swap neurons (the only unlocked dial): 32 → 48 → 64. Each change resets, so Train to 1500 each time. Generalized barely climbs — the extra capacity just memorizes, widening the gap. Overfitting: more neurons ≠ smarter.
