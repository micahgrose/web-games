the myth

"Width and depth are both just 'more brain'."

✗ The myth: adding neurons or layers does the same thing.
✓ The truth: different jobs. Neurons (width) = how much it notices in one stage. Layers (depth) = how many stages it stacks (words → phrases → intent).
👀 Watch: start at 1 layer. Train — auto-pauses at 2000 steps. Note the loss and how blurry / blended the replies are, even with plenty of neurons.
🔁 Raise layers (the only unlocked dial): 1 → 2 → 3. Each change resets, so Train to 2000. At the same width, the loss drops lower and replies hold together — depth buys composition, not size.
Fixed for a fair test: neurons 48 · memory 24 · heads 2 · data 400.
