what each setting does

speed · capacity · quality

Speed — steps per second. It learns faster, not smarter.

neurons — width / capacity. More ≠ smarter: with little data the extra just overfits.

data — the knob where "more" usually helps: more varied greetings raise the generalization ceiling.

creativity / anti-repeat — chat-time: randomness, and how hard it avoids repeating itself.
