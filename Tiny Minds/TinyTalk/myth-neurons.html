the myth

"A bigger network is always better."

✗ The myth: pile on neurons and it gets smarter.
✓ The truth: capacity only helps up to what the data needs. Past that, extra neurons just memorize the training greetings.
👀 Watch: start at neurons 128. Train — auto-pauses at 1500 steps. Memorized races up, generalized lags far behind — the gap is overfitting.
🔁 Swap neurons (the only unlocked dial): 64, then 16. Train to 1500 each time. Generalized doesn't get worse with fewer neurons. More neurons alone don't make it smarter; only more varied data does.
